# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

**Last Updated:** April 22, 2026
**Version:** v1.0.0 (Production)

This Acceptable Use Policy ("Policy") defines the standards for content and behavior when using **UPSYL STUDIO**. Our goal is to maintain a safe, professional, and high-performance environment for all users.

## 1. Prohibited Content
You may not upload, process, or generate any content that:
*   **CSAM**: Involves Child Sexual Abuse Material or anything depicting the sexual exploitation of minors. We have a zero-tolerance policy and will report violations to the appropriate authorities (e.g., NCMEC).
*   **Extreme Violence**: Depicts real-world extreme violence, gore, or cruelty to animals.
*   **Harassment**: Is intended to harass, bully, or defame specific individuals.
*   **Non-Consensual Imagery**: Involves intimate imagery of individuals without their express consent (e.g., "Deepfakes").

## 2. Automated Moderation
To enforce this Policy, we utilize:
*   **Local NSFW Scanners**: All uploads are automatically checked for explicit content using local machine learning models.
*   **Provider Safety Filters**: Our AI partner (Google Vertex AI) implements secondary safety filters. Images rejected by these filters will not be processed, and credits may still be consumed for the attempt.

## 3. Prohibited Behavior
*   **Abuse**: You may not attempt to bypass rate limits or exploit API endpoints.
*   **Jailbreaking**: You may not use specific prompts or techniques ("prompt injection") to force the underlying AI models to generate prohibited content.
*   **Scraping**: Automated scraping of results or system data is strictly prohibited.

## 4. Enforcement
Violations of this Policy may result in:
*   Temporary or permanent account suspension.
*   Forfeiture of all remaining credits without refund.
*   Reporting to legal authorities in cases of illegal content (CSAM).

## 5. Reporting
If you encounter content or behavior that violates this Policy, please report it immediately to: **support@upsyl.studio**
