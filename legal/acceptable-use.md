# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

**Last Updated:** April 21, 2026
**Version:** v1.0.0

To ensure a safe and reliable experience for all users, your use of **Upsyl** is subject to this Acceptable Use Policy.

## 1. Prohibited Content
You may not use our service to process, upload, or generate any content that:
- Is illegal or promotes illegal activities.
- Contains sexually explicit or highly suggestive material (NSFW).
- Is intended to harass, threaten, or harm individuals.
- Infringes upon the intellectual property or privacy rights of others.
- Contains malware, viruses, or harmful code.

## 2. Prohibited Actions
You agree not to:
- Attempt to circumvent our credit system or payment processing.
- Reverse engineer or attempt to exploit our AI processing pipeline.
- Use automated scripts or bots to access the service beyond the limits of the intended user interface.
- Interfere with the stability or security of our server infrastructure.
- Use our service for mass generation of spam or deceitful content.

## 3. Automated Moderation
We use local and cloud-based AI models to detect violations of this AUP.
- **Immediate Rejection**: Images detected as NSFW or otherwise violating will be rejected before processing.
- **Logging**: Violations are logged and may be used for internal audit or account suspension.
- **Enforcement**: Repeated violations will result in permanent account termination and forfeiture of any remaining credits.

## 4. Reporting
If you encounter content generated or hosted by our service that violates this policy, please report it immediately.
