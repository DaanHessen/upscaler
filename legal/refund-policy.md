# Refund Policy

**Last Updated:** April 22, 2026
**Version:** v1.0.0 (Production)

At **UPSYL STUDIO**, we strive to provide the highest quality AI reconstruction services. Because our service involves immediate digital delivery and processing costs, we maintain a strict refund policy.

## 1. Non-Refundable Digital Content
Our Service operates using digital "Units" (Credits). By purchasing credits, you acknowledge and agree that:
*   **Performance Commencement**: Once you Spend or Consume even **one (1) credit** from a purchased pack, the entire transaction becomes **non-refundable**.
*   **Waiver of Withdrawal**: Under EU and international consumer laws regarding digital content, your right of withdrawal (cooling-off period) expires once the performance of the service has begun with your consent.

## 2. Unused Credits
If you have purchased a credit pack but have **not spent any credits**, you are entitled to a full refund within **14 days** of the purchase date. 

## 3. Technical Failures
We understand that technology can occasionally fail. You may request a credit refund or a financial refund in the following scenarios:
*   **System Error**: The Service failed to process your image due to a backend infrastructure error, and no output was provided.
*   **Duplicate Billing**: You were accidentally charged multiple times for the same transaction.

**Note**: Credits will not be refunded for results that you are simply "unhappy with" (e.g., subjective aesthetic preferences), as AI generation is probabilistic and provided "as-is" per our Terms of Service.

## 4. How to Request a Refund
To request a refund for unused credits or a technical failure, please email **support@upsyl.studio** with:
1. Your account email address.
2. The Stripe Transaction ID.
3. A description of the issue (including Job ID if a technical failure occurred).

Refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 5–10 business days after approval.
