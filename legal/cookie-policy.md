# Cookie Policy

**Last Updated:** April 21, 2026
**Version:** v1.0.0

Upsyl uses cookies and similar technologies to ensure our website functions correctly and to provide you with a secure experience.

## 1. What are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They are used to remember your preferences and manage your session.

## 2. Mandatory Functional Cookies
We use "Strictly Necessary" cookies to allow you to use our service:
- **Supabase Auth Tokens**: These cookies identify your session and ensure that only you can access your account and upscale history. These are essential for the operation of the service.
- **Local Storage**: We may use browser local storage to save your UI preferences (e.g., dark mode, interface settings).

## 3. Third-Party Cookies
Our service providers may set cookies during your interaction with their embedded components:
- **Stripe**: Uses cookies to detect and prevent fraud during payment processing.
- **Supabase**: Uses cookies for authentication and session management.

## 4. Managing Your Cookies
Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings. However, **disabling mandatory cookies will prevent you from signing in or using the upscaling service**.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy to reflect changes in our service or regulatory requirements.
